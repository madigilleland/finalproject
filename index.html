Welcome, it's nice to see you!

My name is Madison Gilleland (she/her/hers) and I am currently a sophomore majoring in Vocal Music Education and Creative Advertising at James Madison University, originally from Richmond, Virginia. From a young age, music has been such an integral part of my life. It has such a powerful ability to encourage expression, cultivate community, and inspire hope in the world. My music experience started with an interest in piano, where I soon began taking lessons. Once in middle school, I joined choir and never looked back! I went on to serve as my high school choral department's Tri-M representative, a show captain for the treble show choir, and student director for the first year treble voices ensemble. While my time at JMU is not over yet, I've been able to sing within multiple different ensembles within the choral department and I am also a member of the JMU upper voices a cappella group, Into Hymn.
Upon my graduation in May 2024, I hope to teach middle or high school choir and eventually obtain a graduate or doctoral degree in either Choral Conducting or Music Education. As a music educator, I believe learning never ceases and will strive to continue learning and growing as a musician and educator.
